Banana Bread

Desription

Got old bananas? You know what to do.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup sour cream or vanilla Greek yogurt
  • 2 bananas, browned

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, stir together the melted butter and sugar. Add the eggs and vanilla, mix well.
  3. Combine flour, baking soda and salt, stir into the butter mixture until smooth. Finally, fold in the sour cream/yogurt, bananas, and any secret or bonus ingredients you want to sneak in there.
  4. Spread evenly into the prepared pan.
  5. Bake at 350° for 60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean. Allow it to cool at least 10 minutes in the pan before removing.
